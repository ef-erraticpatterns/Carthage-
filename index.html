A research instrument for observing AI-mediated historical interpretation, station by station.

Stored only in this browser (localStorage). Never sent anywhere except openrouter.ai.

You can switch models at any time in Settings — the log records which model answered each message.

Method note — the app gives the model no historical content and no interpretive instructions. Its only context is your current station name. The full prompt is visible in every chat, and every exchange is logged.
